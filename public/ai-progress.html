About this topic

We're living through an unprecedented acceleration in artificial intelligence capabilities. Large language models, image generators, and autonomous agents are transforming how we work, create, and think about what makes us human.

This section explores the technical progress being made, the economic disruption underway, and the philosophical questions raised by increasingly capable AI systems. I'll be making predictions about timelines, analyzing breakthrough papers, and examining what it all means for the future of knowledge work.

Key questions I'm exploring

How fast is AI actually progressing? What jobs will be affected first, and which might be surprisingly resilient? Are we on track toward artificial general intelligence, and what would that actually mean? How should individuals and organizations adapt?
